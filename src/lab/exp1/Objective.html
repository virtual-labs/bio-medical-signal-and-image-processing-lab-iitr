Digital Signal Processing Toolkit

  • General DSP Functions

Objective:To Study General SP Functions
Selection by User:

  • Selection of appropriate experiment to perform on signals
  • Signal Statics
  • Power Spectrum
  • Convolution/Correlation
  • Selection of corresponding signal type i.e. Sawtooth, Triangular, Square, Sine, Userdefined and Formula based.
  • Selection of Signal Frequency, Amplitude, Phase, DC Bias and simulation period etc.
  • Selection of noise type and standard Deviation
  • Selection of Linear and Circular Convolution type.

Learning Component:

  • To view the nature of Input Signal
  • To study the Statistical characteristics of input signal
  • To study the output waveform by applying single sided and double sided spectrum in Power Spectrum Function
  • Background theory and formulas of function used.
  • Report generation.

 

  • Transforms

Objective: To Study Transform Functions

Selection by User:

  • Selection of appropriate experiment to perform on signal
  • FFT
  • DCT & DST
  • Hilbert Transform
  • Wavelet Transform
  • Chirp-Z Transform
  • Selection of corresponding signal type i.e. Sawtooth, Triangular, Square, Sine, Userdefined and Formula based
  • Selection of Signal Frequency, Amplitude, Phase, DC Bias and simulation period etc.
  • Selection of noise type and standard Deviation
  • Selection of Wavelet type and levels in Wavelet Transform function.

Learning Component:

  • To view the nature of Input Signal
  • To study the fast fourier transform (FFT) and inverse fast fourier transform
  • To study the discrete sine transform and discrete cosine transform
  • To study the Hilbert Transform
  • To study the wavelet transform after applying various wavelet types and levels in Wavelet Transform
  • To study the output waveform by applying Chirp-Z Transform Function
  • Background theory and formulas of functions used above.
  • Report generation.

 

  • Filters

Objectives: To Study Filters Functions
Selection by User:

  • Selection of appropriate experiment to perform on signal
  • IIR
  • Windows Implementation,
  • FIR
  • Signal Filtering,
  • Median Filter,
  • Smoothing Filter
  • Selection of corresponding signal type i.e. Sawtooth, Triangular, Square, Sine, Userdefined and Formula based
  • Selection of Signal Frequency, Amplitude, Phase, DC Bias and simulation period etc.
  • Selection of noise type and standard Deviation
  • Selection of filter type, design method, sampling frequency and ripple specifications in IIR Filter
  • Selection of window type in Window Implementation
  • Selection of Filter type, order, Sampling Frequency, High & Low cutoff Frequency and Standard Deviation.

 

Learning Component:

  • To view the nature of Input Signal
  • To study behavior of Windows Implementation by applying various window types
  • To study the signal filtering by adding or removing noise in Signal Filtering Function
  • To study the median filter with various rank in Median Filter Function
  • Background theory and formulas of functions used above.
  • Report generation.

  • Design DSP

Objectives: To Design DSP
Selection by User:

  • Selection of various operations on all functions given above in concert.
  • Selection of Signal Frequency, Amplitude and Phase
  • Selection of corresponding signal type i.e. Sawtooth, Triangular, Square, Sine, Userdefined and Formula based
  • Selection of Amplitude, Phase, DC Bias and simulation period etc.
  • Selection of required type of window, filters, noise and design method
  • Selection of Sampling Frequency, High & Low cutoff Frequency and Standard Deviation.

Learning Component:

  • To view the nature of Input Signal
  • To analyze difference between all operations have been applied before at one time
  • To study the signal characteristics and waveform in output
  • To study the Phase response and filter order from output
  • Background theory and formulas of functions used above.
  • Report generation.
